# Vision

> The strategic foundation of Moto Routes.

---

## Mission

**Empower motorcyclists to discover and experience the best roads in Portugal and beyond.**

We believe every rider deserves access to verified, high-quality routes - not algorithm-generated paths, but real roads ridden and curated by enthusiasts.

---

## Philosophy

### GPX-First

All route data comes from real GPS files. Every route has been physically ridden and recorded. No synthetic data, no OpenStreetMap automation.

**Why**: Guarantees accuracy. A GPX file is proof that someone actually rode that road.

### Quality Over Quantity

20 perfect routes are worth more than 100 mediocre ones.

**Why**: Motorcyclists don't want to waste a weekend on a disappointing road. Every route we publish must be worth the ride.

### Simplicity

Keep the data pipeline under 500 lines of code. If it's getting complex, we're doing it wrong.

**Why**: Maintainability. One person should be able to understand and modify the entire system.

### Zero Cost

Free tiers only. Supabase free, Vercel free, Mapbox free tier.

**Why**: Sustainability. The project should be able to run indefinitely without funding pressure.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: Weekend Riders

- Age: 30-55
- Ride on weekends and holidays
- Looking for scenic roads near home
- Use smartphone for navigation
- Value: Quick discovery, reliable information

### Secondary: Touring Riders

- Plan multi-day trips
- Need detailed route information
- Download GPX for offline use
- Value: Comprehensive data, journey planning

---

## What We Are

- A curated collection of verified motorcycle routes
- A discovery platform for scenic roads
- A tool for planning motorcycle trips
- Mobile-first, designed for riders on the go

---

## What We Are NOT

- A social network for riders
- A real-time traffic or weather app
- A turn-by-turn navigation app
- A route-creation tool (we curate, not crowdsource)
- A commercial platform with premium features

## Long-term Vision

Phase 1 (MVP): Portugal's best motorcycle roads, curated and verified.

Phase 2: Expand to Spain, France, and other European countries.

Phase 3: Community contributions with editorial review.

**Always maintaining**: Quality over quantity, GPX-first approach.

---

## Design Principles

### Mobile-First

Motorcyclists use their phones. Design for 375px width first, then expand.

### Information Hierarchy

1. What's the route?
2. How long is it?
3. What will I see?
4. How do I get there?

### Instant Value

A user should be able to find and download a route in under 60 seconds.

### Smooth Animations

The map experience should feel premium - smooth fly-to transitions, route drawing animations, responsive interactions.
